About Wildvision.tv

Welcome to Wildvision.tv, your home on the web for short wildlife films. This website isn't just about great footage of wildlife - it's also a place to encounter film makers who are passionate naturalists. Let them introduce you to their favourite subjects and take you on a virtual nature walk. We hope they'll inspire you to head outside and look for the birds, animals and plants they show in these videos.

We plan to update the site regularly with new films, so if you enjoy them and want to find out when there's a new one, please subscribe to our email updates. You can also follow us on Twitter @@WildvisionTV.

And if you're a budding wildlife film maker or presenter, please do get in touch as we'd love to showcase your work here.

There is no company set up for this website, it is individually owned and managed by Paul Brown, a web developer, and Jamie Wyver, a conservationist.

It is currently under development, so there is no registration process yet and the current set of presenters are associates of Paul and Jamie. If you wish to be listed as a presenter, please email editor@@wildvision.tv.

Unless otherwise stated, Paul and Jamie assert full copyright of any data, code, text or binary assets delivered from the Wildvision.tv domain name, content delivery network and host IP address.
